Welcome

Let's take a break and enjoy this incredible voyage to build something innovative. We want this weekend to feel like your event getaway. Climb aboard to join us for a fun weekend of re-designing and much more! While we'd love to have you build something on the theme, our themes are only here for inspiration. As always, build whatever you'd like!

- About Us

CSI Chapter has now collaborated with ACM chapter and Android Club and brought you an wonderful event. CSI CHAPTER is always there here to support students. InnoTech is an innovation competition where students come together to bring a project to life over the course of a weekend. You come up with the idea. Programming experience isn't necessary; the only requirement is a willingness to learn and have fun! Plus, it's FREE!

It's simple!!

Who can register

Anyone who is graduated can participate. Just come in with an open mind and fun ideas to expand on, and you'll be set!

How to Participate

Participate in this 3 round ideathon event. Submit and present you ideas and win exciting prizes

Team

The minimum size of the team must be 2 members. Maximum is 3 members. Team up with the best

Round 1

Participate in a very simple multiple choice coding based questions and get promoted to the next exciting round.

Round 2

In this round you just have to come up with a solution for a real life problem and submit us a report which explains your solution

Round 3

You now have to create small prototype for your solution using any tool of your choice. Incase if it is a product create a presentation
