InnoTech is an innovation competition. This competition is conducted to encourage students with bright ideas and to support them in all ways possible during the process of development.

Reconnecting, piece by piece

Who can register

Anyone who is graduated can participate. Just come in with an open mind and fun ideas to expand on, and you'll be set!

How to Participate

Participate in this 3 round ideathon event. Submit and present you ideas and win exciting prizes

Bang with the Team

The minimum size of the team must be 2 members. Maximum is 3 members. Team up with the best

Round 1

Participate in a very simple multiple choice coding based questions and get promoted to the next exciting round.

Round 2

In this round you just have to come up with a solution for a real life problem and submit us a report which explains your solution

Round 3

You now have to create small prototype for your solution using any tool of your choice. Incase if it is a product create a presentation
